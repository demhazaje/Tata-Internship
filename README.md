# Tata: Data Visualisation - Empowering Business with Effective Insights

## 📌 Project Overview
This repository contains my completed tasks and certification for the Tata Data Visualisation virtual experience on Forage. The project focused on anticipating executive business questions, selecting appropriate visual representations, and delivering actionable insights from raw data to help leadership make informed decisions.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI
* **Data Cleaning:** Data scrubbing and formatting
* **Presentation:** Executive communication and strategic reporting

## 📋 Tasks Completed

### Task 1: Framing the Business Scenario
* Anticipated critical questions for the CEO regarding industry trends, financial health, leadership qualities, and emerging technologies.
* Outlined key considerations for the CMO, including marketing channel optimization, brand identity formulation, and customer data analytics.

### Task 2: Choosing the Right Visuals
* Selected a Line Chart to help the CEO effectively analyze data trends.
* Chose a Stacked Bar Chart for the CMO to view revenue by country and product breakdown simultaneously.
* Utilized a Box Plot for analyzing average revenue metrics and a Column Chart to evaluate the top 10 customers.
* Deployed a Map Chart to clearly illustrate regional demand for the CEO.

### Task 3 & 4: Creating Visuals & Communicating Insights
* Scrubbed the raw dataset to remove inaccurate records, specifically targeting and eliminating rows with negative quantities and unit prices.
* Analyzed revenue trends, global market potential, and consumer purchasing distributions.
* Presented the final analytical findings with strategic recommendations for future geographic growth.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
* **Seasonal Revenue Surges:** Sales from January to August were stable, averaging $685K per month. Revenue grew by 40% in September and peaked at $1.5 million in November, highlighting strong seasonality in the final four months of the year.
* **International Market Opportunities:** Outside of the high-demand UK market, nations such as the Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, and France show significant potential for growth and high sales volume.
* **Untapped Geographic Zones:** Sales are currently heavily concentrated in the European zone. There is a completely untapped market in Africa, Asia, and Russia, which represents an opportunity for a fresh expansion strategy.
* **Healthy Customer Distribution:** The business does not rely heavily on a small handful of buyers. The highest-spending consumer only spent 17% more than the second-highest, indicating limited consumer negotiating power and a positive overall state of business.
