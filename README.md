# Tata: Data Visualisation - Empowering Business with Effective Insights

## 📌 Project Explanation
This project is part of the Tata Data Visualisation virtual experience. The goal was to act as a data consultant helping a retail client’s executive team (CEO and CMO) make data-driven decisions. This involved anticipating their strategic questions, analyzing raw transactional data, and building interactive dashboards to uncover market trends and customer behavior.

## 📋 Requirements
* Anticipate and structure the core business questions the CEO and CMO would ask.
* Select the most effective visual representations for different data types.
* Clean and format a raw retail dataset to ensure accuracy.
* Develop a comprehensive dashboard and present the findings in a professional executive video brief.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI
* **Data Preparation:** Data scrubbing and formatting techniques
* **Presentation:** Executive communication and strategic reporting

## 🚧 Challenges Faced
* **Data Quality Issues:** The raw dataset contained anomalous records, such as negative quantities and unit prices, which required careful scrubbing to prevent skewed analysis.
* **Incomplete Time-Series Data:** The data for December was incomplete, meaning I had to explicitly communicate this limitation to the stakeholders so they wouldn't make false assumptions about end-of-year drop-offs.

## 💡 Key Insights
* **Seasonality:** Revenue is highly seasonal. Sales are stable from January to August (averaging $685K/month) but surge by 40% in September, peaking at $1.5M in November.
* **Geographic Concentration:** Sales are heavily concentrated in the European zone (specifically the UK, Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, and France).
* **Customer Distribution:** The business is healthy and not overly reliant on a few whales; the top customer only spent 17% more than the second-highest.

## 🚀 Recommendations for Improvement
* **Supply Chain Prep:** Inventory and marketing efforts must be heavily scaled up in Q3 to prepare for the massive revenue surges in Q4.
* **Global Expansion:** There is a completely untapped market in Africa, Asia, and Russia. The company should research entry strategies for these regions to diversify revenue streams.
